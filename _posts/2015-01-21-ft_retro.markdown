<div class="text general-margin">
Born during a weekend, Ft Retro was created at <a alt="en.wikipedia.org/wiki/42_(school)" href="https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/42_(school)" target="_blank">42 School</a>
with C++ and <a alt="en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Termcap" href="https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Termcap">termcaps</a>.
As simple as it looks, it was a great challenge to create a small <a alt="en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Shoot'em_up" href="https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Shoot_%27em_up" target="_blank">schmup</a>
in two days. It's build from scratch including basic game logic, ascii sprite display, collisions, movements, enemies, planets and power up.
